My vagina sucks



This website is a collection of stories and resources for anyone who might struggle with their own sexual awareness. It has been designed for anyone suffering from vaginismus, dysperaunia, PTSD and hyctosis, and creates a central location for information lacking in other areas. There is a lot of general information available for women suffering sexually post-menopausal or post-partem, but very little on general discomfort around sexual awareness as a young woman. Vaginas can really suck sometimes -- let's be open about that!
